resume

I spend the most of my time in Photoshop and Illustrator. InDesign? I can do that, too. I’ve designed for web, mobile web, iOS apps, email and print. I benefit from criticism, and can provide leadership and direction.

If needed, I can dust off and further polish my skills in HTML/CSS, Wordpress, 3D modeling, video editing, analytics and pay-per-click campaigns.

I’ve been a Designer for Thrillist Media Group since November 2012. I lead and manage the editorial and marketing requests—continually establishing a design-voice for three of our four content+commerce brands.

When necessary, I dabble in product design (web, mobile & email) and ad sales.

During Summer 2012 I interned as a Designer at InteliSpend. I developed an enticing campaign and set of marketing materials to promote new products and savings during the company’s busiest quarter of the year.

I continually honed my design skills, and further developed my ability to take direction from –and effectively work with– the creative director, senior designers and project managers.

From April 2009 until July 2012 I worked at Mizzou Media as a Designer and Marketing Director. I managed a group of designers to conceptualize and create a department-wide brand identity via promotional materials, including print and social campaigns, contests, and workshops.

Fresh designs gave rise to greater interest in our products, resulting in an increase in profitability for my department.

I spent Summer 2011 at InteliSpend as an Intern Designer. I transformed an old and poorly favored identity into one with renewed vigor; heightening employee satisfaction and enjoyment in the workplace.

Built a base-understanding of following a design process and working in a corporate environment.
